What Is Helldivers 2?

Developed by Arrowhead Game Studios · Released February 8, 2024

Helldivers 2 is a third-person co-op shooter where you and up to three friends are dropped behind enemy lines as elite soldiers of the Helldivers — humanity's last line of defence against alien threats.

Battle across hostile alien planets against the bug-like Terminids and the mechanised Automatons, completing objectives and calling in powerful Stratagems to turn the tide of war.

Every mission contributes to the Galactic War, a live, ever-changing community campaign where millions of players collectively determine the fate of Super Earth.

About Arrowhead Studios

Key Features

Everything that makes Helldivers 2 legendary

🌍

Galactic War

A live, evolving meta-game where the entire playerbase fights together to liberate or defend planets in real time.

🛸

Stratagems

Call in orbital strikes, supply drops, mechs, and more by entering button sequences under fire.

👥

4-Player Co-op

Squad up with friends or matchmake into a team. Coordination — and friendly fire — are both very real.

🎖️

Warbonds

Earn Medals through gameplay to unlock new armour sets, weapons, and cosmetics. No FOMO — Warbonds never expire.

💥

Explosive Combat

Full environmental destruction, realistic ballistics, and a third-person perspective make every firefight intense.

🪐

Diverse Planets

Fight across dozens of procedurally generated biomes — from frozen tundra to volcanic hellscapes.
